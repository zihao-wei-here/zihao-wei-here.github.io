Computational & Integrated Photonic Systems

Zihao Wei

I work at the intersection of integrated photonics, photonic sensing, microwave photonic signal processing, and AI-assisted design.

I am an M.Phil student at the University of Sydney, supervised by Prof. Xiaoke Yi. My research explores how computational methods, optimization, and machine learning can help design, analyze, and interpret complex photonic systems.

Email · GitHub · ORCID

Research Focus

01

Integrated Photonic Sensing

Microring resonators, subwavelength grating structures, resonance shifts, extinction ratio, insertion loss, and sensing-window design.

02

Microwave Photonic Signal Processing

High-resolution spectrum analysis, microwave photonic filters, filter response design, and reconstruction algorithms.

03

Optimization for Photonic Systems

Multi-parameter design, simulation-driven search, Bayesian optimization, evolutionary algorithms, and multi-objective evaluation.

04

AI-assisted Photonic Design

Machine learning for inverse design, sparse signal reconstruction, decoder-based models, and photonic system analysis.
