I am Zihao Wei, an M.Phil student at the University of Sydney, supervised by Prof. Xiaoke Yi.

My background is in Computer Science, and my current research focuses on integrated photonics, photonic sensing, microwave photonic signal processing, and AI-assisted photonic design. I am interested in using computational methods to understand, design, and optimize photonic systems.

## Background

- M.Phil student, University of Sydney
- B.E. in Computer Science, North University of China
- Previous experience at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences

## Research Interests

- Integrated photonic sensing
- Subwavelength grating waveguides and microring resonators
- Microwave photonic filters
- High-resolution spectrum analysis
- Optimization and machine learning for photonic systems
- Signal reconstruction and inverse problems

## Technical Skills

- Photonic simulation: Lumerical FDTD, MODE, INTERCONNECT
- Programming: Python, MATLAB
- Machine learning: PyTorch, model training, signal reconstruction
- Data analysis: spectral post-processing, optimization, visualization
